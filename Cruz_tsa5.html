Selena

S rank - Capriccio

A frame of Selena, more human-like in appearance compared to her Tempest frame, the only mechanical element on Capriccio is the single mechanical arm as her left arm, attached just above the elbow. On her Capriccio frame Selena also has purple eyes and very long straight black hair, inner hair has blue colour, other taggable hairstyle elements are half updo tied by a blue hair ribbon and her hair parts into two at the lower back. Clothing element consist of a black short sleeved shrug, bodystocking covering her back, oblique and armpit areas, and a black and white two-tone short dress with a side slit to the left. She wears a pair of black toeless boots. In her final awakening level her dress becomes a black dress, her bare legs are covered by black pantyhose, and all of the upper bodystocking removed. She uses a weaponised flute in combat, a blade is hidden within the opening end, when depicted it can look like a sword.

Alpha

S rank - Crimson Abyss

Crimson Abyss frame has very long white straight hair, features long sidelocks, swept bangs covers her left eye. Has heterochrome eyes, left iris is grey and right iris is red, both of her arms are mechanical, noticeable robot joints at her wrists, has a pair of headgear attached above her ears that resembles headphones. Clothings are noticeably torn/tattered, wears a red scarf, wears a black vest partially unzipped revealing a center opening window, and a black opened jacket that can be depicted with gradient colors, gradually turning from black to red from top to bottom. Uses a katana as her weapon with a relatively large sheath.ements includes: dog tags, bandaged left leg, bandaid on left cheek, black ankle boots or knee boots, red single thighhigh on right leg (asymmetrical legwear), some of which can be depicted with or without as the apparel changes depending on the playable frame's awakening level.

Vera

S rank - Garnet

Garnet also features very long red hair, but is tied in a single high ponytail, decorated with two black hairbands (the frontal band creates a pair of hair intakes, the second band is placed further up top), bangs between eyes, long sidelocks, and red eyes with a single mole under her left eye. Apparels are typically shown as wearing a skin-tight white bodysuit and a short sleeved jacket, a pair of black thigh high boots. Depending on awakening level, she can be depicted with a black bodysuit and exposed thighs forming zettai ryouiki. Uses a banner spear as her weapon (a polearm, consisting of a banner attached on to a spear).

Luna

S rank - Laurel

Luna, as Agent, has the power and responsibility to choose Contructs and mechanoids to become new Ascendants. Unfortunately, only a few have made it, and the majority have become the Corrupted. Luba used to be saddened by the plight of those Corrupted who have not been blessed by the Ascension-Network. Luna Laurel has long, white hair styled with sidelocks and twintails, with some hair falling between her eyes. She has striking orange eyes with cross-shaped pupils and mechanical legs. Her attire includes a headgear, a floating hair ornament, triangle earrings, a white dress, and a white bodysuit complemented by a thigh strap and a bracelet. She also wields a floating weapon and an orb, adding to her unique and ethereal appearance.

Karenina

S rank - Scire

Karenina has yellow/orange eyes and white hair with her Scire frame, her hair style has twintails at the back, swept bangs and shoulder length sidelocks, overall hair length is medium. The mechanical hair ornaments that ties her twintails together can flip upwards and resemble fake animal ears. All four limbs are mechanical, both of her mechanical arms connects just above the elbow joints, her mechanical legs are hidden well by her thigh boots, exposing only the mechanical knee joints at the back and mechanical feet. The Scire frame has an overall slim appearance with her skin tight black and white bodysuit, the bodysuit itself is of a backless design, with armpit cutouts. The abdomen section of the bodysuit can be depicted with white fabrics instead, she is also sometimes depicted with black pantyhose with thigh cutout design, depending on frame's awakening level. She uses a transformable war hammer as her weapon.
